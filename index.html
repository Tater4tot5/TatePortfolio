About Me

My Story

VIEW MY RESUME

I was born and raised in Austin, Texas (some people call me a unicorn). I've been drawing since before I can remember, and have been designing logos, cards, tshirts and gifts for friends and family for years. After graduating from Southwestern University with a B.A. in Communication Studies, I started a career in Creative Staffing and Recruiting but after working with Lead Designers, Art Directors and creative teams to find design talent, I realized I wanted to be a part of the teams I was helping to build. In 2016, I returned to school to become a UI Designer. Now, my creative skills combined with my experience in Biz Dev and working in the creative industry, give me the unique ability to make informed, functional design choices.

When I'm not doodling or watching Pixar animated movies, you'll find me running around LadyBird lake, swimming laps at Deep Eddy, or sipping on a spicy margarita on Rainey Street.
