About Me

My Story

I was born and raised in Austin, Texas (some people call me a unicorn). I always dreamed of being a cartoonist or children's book illustrator, and after graduating from Southwestern University with a B.A. in Communication Studies, I started a career in Creative Staffing and Recruiting. In 2016, after working with Lead Designers, Art Directors and creative teams to find talent, I realized I wanted to be a part of the teams I was helping build. In 2016, I returned to school full-time to pursue a career in UI Design. I aim to use my illustration and design skills combines with my background in business development and my knowledge of the creative industry to create beautiful, functional web design.

When I'm not doodling in Illustrator, you'll find me running around LadyBird lake, swimming laps at Deep Eddy, or sipping on a spicy margarita at Javelina.
