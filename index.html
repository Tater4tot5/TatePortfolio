About Me

My Story

VIEW MY RESUME

I was born and raised in Austin, Texas (some people call me a unicorn). I've been drawing my whole life, and after winning a coloring contest in first grade, I have since been obsessed with art, design, drawing, color theory, painting and illustration. In 8th grade I designed a T-shirt for my youth group and taught myself to use (the original) Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop. I have been designing logos, cards, tshirts and gifts for friends and family ever since. Upon graduating from Southwestern University with a B.A. in Communication Studies, I started a career in Creative Staffing and Recruiting. After four years of working with Lead Designers, Art Directors and Creative Teams to define and fill UI/UX and Front-End Development roles, I decided to pursue a Design career in this new and evolving field. Now, my design and illustration skills combined with my experience in Biz Dev and working in the creative industry, give me the unique ability to make informed, functional design choices.

When I'm not doodling or watching Bob's Burgers, you'll find me running around LadyBird lake, swimming laps at Deep Eddy Pool, or sipping on a fromar (frozen Margarita) at Matt's El Rancho.
